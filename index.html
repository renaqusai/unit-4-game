Crystals Collector

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Use the crystals to match your points to the random number. The value of the crystals changes for every game and you must figure out their values on your own.

Match your points to the random number to win. If you go over, you lose!

Each time when the game starts, the value of each crystal will be random.
